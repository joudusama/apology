Apology Letter

Dear Khalid,

In the realm of apologies, where words meet regret,

I weave a verse, a poetic duet.

Stress, a typo in life's grand play,

A moment misspelled, causing dismay.

Yet let this poem be a sweet serenade,

To mend the rift my mistake has made.

With love as the ink, forgiveness the quill,

Our story continues, as it always will.

Sorry for the stress, my dear Khalid,

In the poetry of life, let love be our ballad.
